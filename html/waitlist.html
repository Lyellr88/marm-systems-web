Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining the MARM waitlist

When will MARM be available?

The dual-RAG memory system is currently in development. Waitlist members will get first access to the beta version, expected in Q3 2025. We believe in shipping when it's ready, not when it's rushed.

What makes MARM different from ChatGPT or Claude?

We're not competing with them, we're complementing them. MARM provides the continuous memory layer that all AI interactions need. Think of it as the memory infrastructure that makes every AI smarter, not a replacement for existing models.

What's the lifetime pricing offer?

The first 100 waitlist members who convert to paid users get lifetime access at the launch price ($10/month becomes lifetime). This locks in your pricing forever, no increases, no surprises.

How do I know my data is safe?

You control what gets remembered. Manual session saves, monthly memory reviews, and complete transparency in what's stored. We ask permission before remembering anything, ensuring full user consent and control.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Your memory data exports with you, and there are no cancellation fees. We believe if you want to leave, you should be able to with all your data intact.
